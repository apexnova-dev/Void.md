# 🌑 Void.md Branding Protocol
>
> **Standard Operating Visuals & Voice**

## 1. The Core Philosophy

**The Void** represents the absence of the "Cloud." The branding must reflect a sense of **Technical Sovereignty** and **Minimalist Power**.

## 2. Visual Identity (Neon City Aesthetic)

The visual language is "Cyber-Terminal"—modern, high-contrast, and optimized for late-night productivity.

### Color Palette

* **Void Black:** `#0a0b10` (Background)
* **Electric Cyan:** `#00f2ff` (Primary Action)
* **Cyber Magenta:** `#ff00ea` (Secondary Accents)
* **Neon Purple:** `#7b2eff` (System Overlays)

### Typography

* **Primary:** Monospace (Fira Code / JetBrains Mono) for that "Terminal" feel.

## 3. Voice & Tone

The voice is a **Skeptical Technical Authority**. It is direct, professional, and slightly "hacker-chic."

* **Keywords:** *Protocol, Grid, Interface, Sovereignty, Triage, Matrix, Handshake.*
* **The Mantra:** "Void.md: 'No Cloud', just the local Void."

## 4. Logo Usage

The logo is a geometric **V** icon encased in a dashed "Void Ring."

* **Gradient:** Cyan to Magenta.

## 5. Credits & Signature

Every official Void.md deployment or documentation must conclude with the production stamp:

> **Void.md — A Neon City Production**
