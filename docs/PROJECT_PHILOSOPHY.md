## The Philosophy

In a world where every application demands to sync your data to their servers, **Void.md** proudly claims the local Void as its home.

*"Void.md: 'No Cloud', just the local Void."*

No tracking. No telemetry. Just you, your machine, and the Void.

---

## Data Sovereignty Principles

* **100% Local:** Your data never leaves your disk.
* **Air-Gap Ready:** Works perfectly without an internet connection.
* **Git-Native:** Your board is versionable, diffable, and syncable over private infrastructure.
* **Zero Network Footprint:** The app makes no outbound connections.
